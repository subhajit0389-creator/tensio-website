About Tensio

Engineering Precision. Creative Solutions. Complete Delivery.

Who We Are

Tensio Design & Civil Consultancy Pvt. Ltd. is a multidisciplinary engineering and design consultancy based in India, offering comprehensive services in tensile structures, interior design, civil contracting, property audits, and manpower support.

Founded by two professionals with distinct expertise — one from the **fashion design industry** bringing aesthetic sensibility and deep understanding of materials, and the other from a **civil engineering background** with a strong foundation in technical execution and project management — Tensio unites creativity and structural excellence.

After years of contributing behind the scenes to multiple organizations, the founders envisioned a brand that values both technical accuracy and customer empathy. Thus, **Tensio** was born — an independent entity built to demonstrate capability, transparency, and innovation in every project.
